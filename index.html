After graduating from Boise State with a BBA in Marketing, I entered into the elusive technology start-up scene in my hometown of Seattle. Brought on as a Marketing Assistant, I quickly took over a growing amount of responsibilities from the 3-person marketing team at BlueKai. When our company was then bought out by tech-giant Oracle, my small event background at BlueKai ushered me into the world of planning technology conferences for the newly formed Oracle Marketing Cloud. While the experience was invaluable, I quickly realized that I was not being challenged enough, nor did I enjoy the culture of the marketing world.

Having worked right next to the software engineers and developers for several years, I was able to learn more about what they do on a day to day basis as well as what they look for when hiring on to their team. Taking on this new career change I knew would be challenging but also would be best for me long-term both career wise and personally. With 9 months down in the Web Development program at Seattle University, I can confirm that this was the best choice I could have made. Being challenged on a regular basis is something I expect and look forward to!

While I am trained as a Web Developer, I work with an emphasis on user experience design and execution. Creating websites that are both easy to use as well as visually appealing is one of my passions that I look forward to sharing with my future employer and team.
